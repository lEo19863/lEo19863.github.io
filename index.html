My recommended drink is the milk tea with golden bubbles.

At sugar level 50%.

Golden bubbles are made from tapioca and it is a special bubble served at Koi the. Unlike normal boba, it has a unique golden, transparent glow and a very chewy texture. They are quite small and easy to swallow.

The milk tea is a mix of Chinese tea and milk. it doesn't have a strong accent, but it is flavourful and bittersweet. It creates a harmony when it is topped off with golden bubbles because it adds more sweetness and texture.
